# Levant Tech Solutions - HR & Project Management KPI Brief

## KPI 1: Department Salary Expenditure (Budget Tracking)
* **Name:** Total Salary Expenditure by Department
* **Definition:** Calculates the total monthly salary burden for each department (derived from Q2).
* **Current value:** Engineering has the highest expenditure at $938,000, followed by Research at $558,000.
* **Interpretation:** This indicates that Engineering and Research are the company's largest investments. HR and Finance should monitor these departments to ensure the output matches the high financial allocation.

## KPI 2: Employee Utilization Rate
* **Name:** Unassigned Talent Count
* **Definition:** Identifies the number of employees currently not assigned to any active projects (derived from Q7).
* **Current value:** (Value depends on the Q7 query results, typically representing "benched" employees).
* **Interpretation:** A high number of unassigned employees means the company is paying salaries without getting direct project value. Management should immediately assign these employees to under-staffed projects or internal training (Certifications).

## KPI 3: Salary Equity & Benchmarking
* **Name:** Above-Average Departments Index
* **Definition:** Compares the average salary of each department against the overall company average (derived from Q5).
* **Current value:** Departments identified in Q5 have an average salary higher than the company baseline.
* **Interpretation:** This helps HR ensure pay equity. If a non-technical department has an unusually high average salary compared to the company average, it may require an audit to ensure fair compensation practices across the organization.
